## About

I deliver interactive workshops and lectures on **Artificial Intelligence**, **Blockchain Technology**, and their powerful convergence. My sessions combine cutting-edge technical content with hands-on demonstrations, designed for audiences ranging from graduate students to industry professionals.

**Core thesis:** *AI is giving agency and intelligence to blockchain systems, and blockchain is giving trust and transparency to AI systems.*

## Core Topics

### Agentic Workflows & Autonomous Systems
Understanding AI-driven autonomy and how agents execute tasks, make decisions, and collaborate.

### Large Language Models
Deep dive into GPT-4, Claude, DeepSeek, Gemini - architectures, capabilities, and practical applications.

### AI-Powered Information Retrieval
From Perplexity to RAG systems - how modern AI accesses and synthesizes knowledge.

### Web3 × AI Convergence
How AI enhances blockchain development, security, and user experience - and how blockchain provides trust infrastructure for AI.

### Prompt Engineering & Best Practices
Practical techniques for getting the most out of AI systems.
