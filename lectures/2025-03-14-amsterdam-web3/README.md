# Web3 Amsterdam Summit Keynote

**Date:** March 10-16, 2025 (Keynote ~March 14)
**Event:** Web3 Amsterdam Summit
**Location:** Amsterdam, Netherlands
**Type:** Conference Keynote

---

## Overview

International keynote presentation at the Web3 Amsterdam Summit, presenting to a global audience of blockchain developers, founders, and investors on how AI serves as an enabler for Web3 adoption and development.

## Presentation: "AI as the Web3 Enabler"

### Core Thesis
"Web3 is incomplete without AI" - AI addresses critical barriers in Web3 adoption including user complexity, security vulnerabilities, and lack of automation.

### Topics Covered

- The Problem with Current Web3
- AI's Role as an Enabler
  - Smart contract writing & auditing
  - Autonomous agents for transaction management
  - Fraud detection
  - On-chain AI models
- Emerging Technologies & Threats
  - Autonomous AI agents
  - Cryptocurrency as "robot money"
  - Scam agents and security concerns
- Quantum Computing Integration
- Call to Action for builders
