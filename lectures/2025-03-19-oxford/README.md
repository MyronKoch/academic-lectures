# Oxford University Blockchain Society Lecture

**Date:** March 19, 2025
**Institution:** Oxford University
**Host:** Oxford Blockchain Society
**Audience:** Students with blockchain experience, some with extensive development backgrounds

---

## Overview

Comprehensive lecture at Oxford covering the convergence of AI and blockchain technologies. The audience included some of the world's future leaders - exceptional students with varying levels of blockchain and development experience.

## Presentation: "Convergence of AI and Blockchain - Driving the Future"

### Core Thesis
"AI is giving agency and intelligence to blockchain systems, and blockchain is giving trust and transparency to AI systems."

### Topics Covered

1. **Agentic Workflows** - Autonomous AI agents executing tasks on blockchain with safety guardrails
2. **Large Language Models** - Comparing GPT-4 and Claude for blockchain applications
3. **AI-Powered Information Retrieval** - Tools like Perplexity and SCIRA for accessing knowledge
4. **DeepSeek** - Advanced reasoning AI models for complex problem-solving
