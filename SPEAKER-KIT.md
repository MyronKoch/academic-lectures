## Bio

Myron Koch is an educator and technologist specializing in the intersection of artificial intelligence and blockchain technology. He has delivered workshops and lectures at leading institutions including **Harvard**, **MIT**, **Oxford**, **Princeton**, **Cornell Tech**, and **NYU**, as well as international conferences like **Web3 Amsterdam**.

His teaching style emphasizes hands-on demonstrations, real-time tool exploration, and practical applications. Myron's sessions are known for transforming complex technical concepts into accessible, engaging content that leaves attendees with actionable knowledge and curated resource lists for continued learning.

## Speaking Topics

### 1. AI Fundamentals & Modern LLMs
*Duration: 60-120 minutes*

An accessible introduction to large language models, covering architecture, training pipelines, and the differences between major models (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek). Includes live demonstrations.

**Ideal for:** General audiences, business students, non-technical professionals

### 2. Agentic Workflows & AI Agents
*Duration: 90-120 minutes*

Deep dive into autonomous AI agents - what they are, how they work, and the frameworks powering them (LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, Flowise). Covers the spectrum from no-code to full code implementations.

**Ideal for:** Developers, technical students, AI practitioners

### 3. AI Ã— Blockchain Convergence
*Duration: 90-150 minutes*

Exploring how AI enhances blockchain (smart contract auditing, security, UX) and how blockchain provides trust infrastructure for AI (decentralized compute, verifiable inference). The future of Web3 + AI.

**Ideal for:** Blockchain clubs, Web3 conferences, crypto-native audiences

### 4. Prompt Engineering Masterclass
*Duration: 60-90 minutes*

Practical techniques for effective AI interaction - from basic prompting to advanced chain-of-thought reasoning, few-shot learning, and system prompt design.

**Ideal for:** Anyone working with AI tools, content creators, developers

### 5. Custom Workshop
*Duration: Flexible*

Tailored content combining any of the above topics based on your audience's needs and interests.
